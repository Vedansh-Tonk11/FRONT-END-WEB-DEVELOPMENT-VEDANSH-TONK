Microscope Basics

Microscope Parts and Functions

  • Eyepiece: Where you look through to view the specimen (typically 10x magnification)
  • Objective Lenses: Provide primary magnification (4x, 10x, 40x typical)
  • Stage: Platform where the slide is placed
  • Stage Clips: Hold the slide in place
  • Focus Knobs: Coarse and fine adjustment for focusing
  • Light Source: Illuminates the specimen from below

Proper Microscope Usage

  1. Always start with the lowest power objective (4x or 10x)
  2. Place the slide on the stage and secure with stage clips
  3. Use coarse focus to bring specimen into general focus
  4. Use fine focus for precise focusing
  5. Adjust light intensity for optimal viewing
  6. Switch to higher power objectives as needed
  7. Never use coarse focus with high power objectives
